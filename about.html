Hello, I'm Lucian Marin. I have a background in electronics engineering and currently doing what I love, designing and building awesome products. I strongly think that design is all about making impossible possible. I dare to like meticulous design, modern typography, electronic music and everything in between.

I designed and build The Journalist — a very popular WordPress theme, I was interviewed by Digerati — the Romanian version of The Setup, I was featured on the official Google blog for my work on their favicon, I have been part of team who wrote the Newscoop 3 Cookbook, I have been interviewed and published in the Icon Handbook about a peculiar project and probably did some other cool stuff that you may never hear about.

About the site

The site is created using the latest web innovations. It is statically generated with Jekyll and Saas. It is hosted by Rohost — probably the best hosting provider from Romania. For all typographic necessities I'm using Vegur typeface. The complete source code of the site is made available on GitHub.

Some things on the site are written in 日本語 because I'm a fan of Japanese culture and art. I don't know the language, but I hope one day I get the chance to learn it.
