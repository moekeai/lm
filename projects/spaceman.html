Spaceman, or redefinition of modernism, is a smart, sophisticated Jekyll theme designed for professionals.

Theme setup with Jekyll

After you download the theme, all you need to do is jekyll serve -w in terminal. Then open your favorite text editor and edit _config.yml with your details. The three posts that come with the theme can be edited or new ones can be added using the same structure.

The theme is licensed under the MIT license. Basically, you can do the same things with the theme as with Jekyll, since both have the same license.
