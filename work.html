Markdawn — CMS 2013

Markdawn is one of the most amazing project I was working on. A publishing platform developed and designed entirely by me. I've written about it as a dawn of a new era freeing people from the WordPress nightmare.

Aerogram — Newsletter 2013

Aerogram is an alternative to MailChimp and CampaignMonitor. It's fast, it's light, it's pretty and it will be soon available. So, please go ahead and sign up and meet us on the other side.

Lira Media — Apps suite 2013

Lira Media is an unique concept of email marketing. Designed not only to delight the receiver of promotions, but also the marketer. Screenshots from the app can be seen on Behance: Lira Suite, Lira Delivery and the iPhone version.

Subway Map — Bucharest 2013

I redesigned the subway map of Bucharest to easily get from point A to point B in a city that never sleeps. The page is iPhone and Android friendly. You can even pinch and zoom on the map.

Moss — OS Experiment 2012

Moss wants to be an experimental OS that can handle new technologies and promise to be on desktop as simple as iOS is on mobile. Everybody deserves power and simplicity on a big monitor screen.

Responsive Design 2011

One of my last addiction is to make all the sites I design compatible with both iPhone and iPad. The results are speaking for themselves. There are more pictures from this project on Behance and Dribbble.

BitDefender UI 2011

BitDefender is an antivirus software company from Romania. I’ve applied for a job at the company and above is the result of the interview. I was lucky, they didn’t choose me.

Feews 2010

I’ve provided the entire user experience of Feews, a brand new kind of feed reader. Simplicity and easy of use were the top priorities for the design process. Of course, the implementation was done by me based on latest web standards.

Ecobat Construct 2010

Ecobat Construct is a Bruxelles based construction company. When I started designing the site I was thinking that the user experience should be like the modern architectural style: minimalist, yet innovative and focused on people.

Magazin De Locuinte 2007

Magazin De Locuinte was the first 360 degrees experience I was working on. I’ve designed the entire branding platform: the user interface for the site, the identify, the business cards, a map and even some posters.
