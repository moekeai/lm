Hi there. I'm Lucian Marin.

I have a background in electronics engineering and currently doing what I love, designing and building amazing products. I dare to like meticulous design, modern typography, electronic music and everything in between. I strongly think that design is all about making impossible possible.

I designed and build The Journalist — a very popular WordPress theme, I was interviewed by Digerati — the Romanian version of The Setup, I was featured on the official Google blog for my work on their favicon, I have been part of team who wrote the Newscoop 3 Cookbook, I have been interviewed and published in the Icon Handbook about a peculiar project and probably did some other cool stuff that you may never hear about.

In 2013 I started working on Markdawn and I'm still trying to figure out how to do publishing right. In 2014 I built Sublevel, a beautiful engineered network and Monochrome, a beautiful engineered browser.

About the site

The site is created using the latest web innovations then statically generated with Jekyll and Sass. It is hosted by Rohost — probably the best hosting provider from Romania.

Some things are written in 日本語 because I'm a fan of Japanese culture and art. I don't know the language, but I hope I get the chance to learn it one day.
