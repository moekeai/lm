After you download the theme, all you need to do is jekyll serve -w in terminal. Then open your favorite text editor and edit _config.yml with your details. The three posts that come with the theme can be edited or new ones can be added using the same structure.

The theme is licensed under the MIT license. Basically, you can do the same things with the theme as with Jekyll, since both have the same license. I do have a small request: If you do use the theme, could you send a link back this way? Of course, there is no obligation to do this other than professional courtesy but I would definitely appreciate the gesture. :-)

The theme will always stay free, but donations are very much appreciated and will help continuing the development and to provide support — donate via PayPal.
